This Privacy Policy explains how SeaPilotApp (“the app”) handles information.

What the app does

The app is a navigation and AIS viewer. It can receive AIS/NMEA data over Wi‑Fi (TCP/UDP) or Bluetooth, and can show the device’s GPS position on a map.

Data the app processes

  • Location (GPS): used to show your position and related navigation info. Processed on-device.
  • AIS/NMEA data: received from your AIS gateway/device. Processed on-device.
  • App logs (optional): if you use the “Save log” feature, logs are stored locally on your device.

What the app collects and shares

The app does not require an account and does not sell personal data.

The app may use Firebase Crashlytics to improve stability. Crash reports can include device information (such as OS version, device model) and technical crash diagnostics. We do not intentionally send AIS content or your precise location to Crashlytics.

Online maps and third‑party services

If you enable online map layers, your device connects to the selected map/tile provider to download map tiles. The provider may receive standard technical data such as your IP address and request metadata. Please review the provider’s privacy policy.

Permissions

  • Location — to show GPS position and related navigation data.
  • Nearby devices / Bluetooth — to connect to Bluetooth AIS gateways (if you use this mode).
  • Network (Wi‑Fi/Internet) — to connect to AIS over TCP/UDP and load online map tiles (if enabled).
  • Storage — to store offline maps and saved logs (if you use these features).

Data retention

Saved logs and offline map files stay on your device until you delete them (or uninstall the app).

How to delete data

You can delete saved logs and offline files from the app (where available) or from your device storage. Uninstalling the app removes its local data.

Children

The app is not intended for children under 13.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy. The “Effective date” above will be updated.
